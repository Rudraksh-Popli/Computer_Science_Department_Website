"MMDU's Computer Science Department aims to spark innovation and build the next generation of computer engineers"


The Department of Computer Science stands as a center of excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Committed to academic rigor and technological advancement, the department offers comprehensive programs that equip students with a strong foundation in computing principles and emerging technologies.

Our curriculum is designed to foster analytical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills through a balanced combination of theoretical knowledge and practical experience. Supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and modern computing facilities, students gain exposure to diverse domains such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, software engineering, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

The department takes pride in its distinguished faculty, cutting-edge research initiatives, and strong industry collaborations that prepare graduates to excel in academia, research, and professional practice. We continue to inspire innovation, encourage inquiry, and shape the next generation of leaders in the field of computer science.
